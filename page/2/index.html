Superconductivity Lab

Superconductivity Lab was established  at School of Physical Sciences, National Institute of Science Education and Research (NISER) Bhubaneswar. Superconductivity has been an exotic property of materials due to their unique charge and magnetic transport properties. Thus, they offer a great potential for applications in quantum computation, detectors, spintronic devices, levitated transport etc.  Here, we intend to study the physics of various superconducting/superconducting hybrid interfaces, nanostructures and devices.
