  • Spin-filter Josephson Junction


Most prefered material for spin current generation from spin imbalance in the band structure can be in a spin filter with a high spin filter efficiency. Spin filters in a josephson junction can reveal the feature of getting triplet or as an insulating ferromagnetic barrier for studying higher order harmonics also. Keeping all aspects open about it, modification or in combination with other materials, we can study the more reliable part of superconducting spintronics by combining ferromagnetism, spin orbit interaction and superconducting physics.

  • Spin-glass Josephson Junction

The non equilibrium physics of a spin glass can not be fully understood until getting an idea about its energy state or any configurational state. Its randomness and frustration of spins make it more interesting. Since josephson junction is a very phase sensitive tool, one can put non equilibrium systems as a barrier inside it. The results from those devices will be rich and unputdownable in terms of superconductivity and spin glass physics.

  • Spin-orbit coupling induced effects in Josephson Junctions


The ferromagnetic material in a barrier while studying with singlet josephson junction becomes restricted as a function of its proximatisation length scale and its magnetisation increases complexity in making squid devices from it.  The righteous choice in a josephson junction is spin orbit interaction  by which we can generate, manipulate and control the spin near a junction area without having any remnants.
